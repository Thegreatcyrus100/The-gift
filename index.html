For You

A little page made with all my heart.

Dear Love,

I don’t want this to sound like something copied from a movie or written just to sound pretty. I just want it to sound like me — like how I feel when I think about you. You make my life softer. Not in a weak way, but in a way that feels safe. Like I don’t have to pretend, or be “on,” or prove anything. With you, I can just exist. And that means more to me than I probably say out loud. I love the little things about you. The way you react when you’re excited. The way your voice changes when you’re tired. The random thoughts you share that somehow make my whole day better. You probably don’t even realize how much those small things matter to me — but they do. A lot. You’ve become part of my everyday thinking. When something funny happens, I want to tell you first. When something good happens, I want you there. And when things feel heavy, you’re the person I wish I could sit next to in silence. I’m not perfect. I don’t always say things the best way. I get stubborn sometimes. But what I feel for you is real and steady. It’s not just butterflies — it’s comfort, loyalty, and wanting to build something that lasts. I don’t just love you when it’s easy. I love you when we disagree, when we’re tired, when life feels messy. I love you in the normal, boring, everyday moments. Those are the moments that matter most to me. You’re not just my girlfriend. You’re my person. And I’m really, really grateful I get to love you.

— With all my heart
